# ✅ LTI MIND TREE Interview Questions List

1. Can you provide an introduction about yourself?

2. What is your total years of experience, and how many years have you worked on SAP CPI?

3. What technologies did you work on for the remaining years of your experience?

4. Are you certified?

5. What is your current organization and your current location?

6. Are you working on a development project or a support project?

7. What adapters have you worked with in SAP CPI?

8. Can you explain the configuration details required for an SFTP adapter?

9. What Camel expression would you use to exclude a specific file while fetching files from a directory?

10. How do you configure an IDoc adapter in SAP CPI?

11. Do you know what partner profiles are in SAP?

12. How do you check for duplicate messages in SAP CPI?

13. What is a Looping Process Call, and when would you use it?

14. What is the difference between OData V2 and OData V4? What file formats does OData V2 support?

15. If you want to send a message to multiple receivers, how would you design the integration flow?

16. What is Multicast in SAP CPI, and what are its types?

17. In a Parallel Multicast scenario, if one branch expects CSV and another expects XML, will processing wait or execute independently?

18. How does the behavior change in Sequential Multicast?

    
19. One Message need to send to multiple Recivers , how you will send?

 
20. How strong are your skills in Groovy scripting?

21. Can you review a given Groovy script and summarize what it does?

22. What Camel expression is used to get the current date and time?

23. What Camel expression is used for exception handling and logging errors?

24. Can you explain your current project?

25. Can you describe one critical integration flow, including palette elements and adapters used?

26. Do you have any questions for us? (e.g., about the project or business requirements)
