Here are the interview questions asked during the sessions, formatted as a numbered list:

1. Can you help me to understand your **expertise towards BTPIS** and your previous organization?
2. How many **end-to-end implementations** have you done?
3. What are the various **integration patterns** that you have worked on so far?
4. What do you mean by an **integration pattern**?
5. If you have a business use case where **multiple receiver resources** are targeted from a single S4 system, what is your integration pattern?
6. Have you heard about **synchronous and asynchronous**, and what do they mean?
7. Apart from synchronous and asynchronous, what are the other **integration patterns** that you are aware of?
8. If I have an **OData service**, how do you design a flow for it?
9. How do you handle **pagination** and multiple records in an OData service?
10. What are the details that you would need when you are connecting to an **OData service**?
11. What kind of **palette functions** have you worked on?
12. What does a **content modifier** do, and how do you write the expression to set a header?
13. How do you **externalize variables** (like an endpoint URL or port number), and how do you technically define the alias ID?
14. Have you worked on **value maps**, and how do you actually create a value mapping using CPI?
15. Have you worked on any other **BTP capabilities**?
16. What is **multicast**, and what is the difference between parallel and sequential multicast?
17. When do you use a **JMS adapter**, and if you are decoupling, can you explain technically how you do it?
18. When do you use a **process call**, and how do you define it?
19. How do you monitor and analyze the capabilities of **SAP API Management** if a proxy might fail?
20. What kind of **API policies** have you worked on so far?
21. How do you implement **IP whitelisting**?
22. How do you handle a wide range of IP addresses (like an Azure application with a 1,000 to 10,000 range) in your **IP whitelisting** policy?
23. How do you define **spike arrest** or rate limiting?
24. What is **XSUAA**, and how is it responsible for security in API management?
25. What **security mechanisms** and authentications have you implemented so far for your integration flows or APIs?
26. How do you design your flow to fetch a **refresh token** before sending a response to a target system?
27. What is the use case for the **Event Mesh**?
28. Which authentications have you used with **SuccessFactors adapter connections**?
29. What is the procedure and what details are required to create **client credentials** in CPI?
30. What is the difference between the **Compound Employee API** and OData in SuccessFactors?
31. What data can we pull by using the SOAP service **Compound Employee API**?
32. How do you handle **pagination** when fetching a large volume of data in SuccessFactors, and do you need any expressions in the query?
33. How can we implement a **delta sync** manually to fetch the delta records instead of a full load?
34. If you are calling a SuccessFactors API and getting an **HTTP 403 Forbidden error**, what might be the issue, and how is it different from a 401 error?
35. What authentication methodologies have you used in the **SFTP adapter**?
36. Do you have any idea how to configure a **public key** in CPI for SFTP?
37. Have you used **dual authentication** in SFTP?
38. If you get an error that a **certificate was expired**, how do you test and upload the new certificate?
39. Have you worked on **API Management**?
40. What is the basic difference between an **API Provider** and an **API Proxy**?
41. If you have an SAP S4 system as a data provider and a third-party application (like Google) as a consumer, how will you create an **API proxy endpoint**?
42. For creating the **API Provider**, do you need a policies template or what specific data is required?
